How to remove the "Made on Tilda" credit from the footer.
The "Made on Tilda" credit is placed on all website pages by default. However, you can remove it in the Site Settings if you have an active annual Tilda Personal Plan or one of the annual Tilda Business Plan subscriptions.
If you have such a subscription, go to the Site Settings → More → Platform Label → Off. Save changes and republish all pages.
